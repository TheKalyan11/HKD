About

Vrindavan Chandrodaya Mandir

We aspire to create a world-class and iconic monument for Sri Krishna in the holy land of Vrindavan in pursuance of Srila Prabhupada's direction. Vrindavan Chandrodaya Mandir will place Krishna proudly on the world mindscape and Vrindavan on the world map. This project is dedicated to playing a larger role in shaping the future of India.

About

Vrindavan Chandrodaya Mandir

We aspire to create a world-class and iconic monument for Sri Krishna in the holy land of Vrindavan in pursuance of Srila Prabhupada's direction. Vrindavan Chandrodaya Mandir will place Krishna proudly on the world mindscape and Vrindavan on the world map. This project is dedicated to playing a larger role in shaping the future of India.
